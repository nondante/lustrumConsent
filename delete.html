Lustrum - Account Deletion

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Lustrum app
  2. Go to Profile (tap your profile icon)
  3. Tap "Settings"
  4. Scroll to bottom and tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm deletion

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account credentials
  • All saved tarot readings
  • Journal entries and notes
  • Purchase history (for our records only)
  • All personal data

Data Retention

All data is deleted immediately upon account deletion. Purchase records are retained for 7 years as required by law for tax purposes only.

Alternative: Email Request

You can also request account deletion by emailing: lustrum.dev@gmail.com
